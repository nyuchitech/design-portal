## Three Sources of Truth

Two platform-owned. One person-owned. Everything else derives from these three.

<div className="mt-4 grid gap-4 sm:grid-cols-3">
  <Card>
    <CardHeader className="pb-2">
      <CardTitle className="text-base">Supabase / PostgreSQL</CardTitle>
    </CardHeader>
    <CardContent>
      <Badge variant="outline" className="mb-2">Platform — relational</Badge>
      <p className="text-xs text-muted-foreground">Structured data with schemas, foreign keys, and Row Level Security. Identity, places, events, commerce, news metadata, wallet.</p>
    </CardContent>
  </Card>
  <Card>
    <CardHeader className="pb-2">
      <CardTitle className="text-base">ScyllaDB</CardTitle>
    </CardHeader>
    <CardContent>
      <Badge variant="outline" className="mb-2">Platform — non-relational</Badge>
      <p className="text-xs text-muted-foreground">All content and all streams. Article bodies, messages, AI conversations, creator content. Wide-column storage on Fly.io (Johannesburg).</p>
    </CardContent>
  </Card>
  <Card>
    <CardHeader className="pb-2">
      <CardTitle className="text-base">Web3 Pod</CardTitle>
    </CardHeader>
    <CardContent>
      <Badge variant="outline" className="mb-2">Personal — sovereign</Badge>
      <p className="text-xs text-muted-foreground">Your sovereign data store. Preferences, Digital Twin memory, AI context. Cryptographically bound to your MIT token. We never see it.</p>
    </CardContent>
  </Card>
</div>

<p className="mt-4 text-sm text-muted-foreground">
  <strong className="text-foreground">Speed is rented. Truth is owned.</strong> Edge caches (Cloudflare KV, Durable Objects)
  and device caches (RxDB/SQLite) can be rebuilt from the three sources of truth in minutes.
  CouchDB is the sync protocol — the pipe, not the warehouse.
</p>
